Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats."

prepration time

  • Total: Approximaly 10 minutes
  • Prepration : 5 minutes
  • Cooking : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs : In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a lighter, fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan : Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette : Once the butter has melted and starts bubbling, pour in the beaten eggs. Tilt the pan slightly to spread the eggs evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional) : When the eggs begin to set around the edges but are still slightly runny in the center, sprinkle your chosen fillings (cheese, vegetables, cooked meats, etc.) over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve : As the omelette finishes cooking, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Fold and serve: Serve hot, and add extra salt and pepper if desired
